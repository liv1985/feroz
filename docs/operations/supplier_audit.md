## Sampling Audit Checklist
1. **Material Thickness**: Measure with calipers (0.4mm is standard for dresses).
2. **Seam Integrity**: Check for glue residue or bubbling.
3. **Zipper Quality**: Ensure YKK or equivalent heavy-duty zippers are used.
4. **Odor**: High-quality latex should have a mild rubber scent, not a strong chemical smell.

## Fulfillment Strategy
- **Initial Phase**: Stock in-house in Canada (Ship via Canada Post/Chit Chats).
- **Packaging**: Use discreet, acid-free tissue paper and branded dust bags.
- **Customs**: Ensure "HS Code 4015.90" (Rubber apparel) is used for imports to Canada.
