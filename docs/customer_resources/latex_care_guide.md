# Feroz Fashion - Latex Care & Maintenance Guide

*Thank you for choosing Feroz Fashion. To ensure your latex garment lasts for years, please follow these essential care instructions.*

## 1. Dressing
- **Avoid Sharp Objects**: Latex is easily punctured by fingernails or jewelry.
- **Lubrication**: NEVER attempt to put on dry latex. Use a silicone-based lubricant (dressing aid) on your skin or a light dusting of talc/cornstarch inside the garment.
- **Avoid Oils**: Keep latex away from oil-based products (moisturizers, perfumes, massage oils) as they degrade the material.

## 2. Shining
- Latex arrives in a "matte" state. To achieve a high-gloss look, apply a specialized silicone-based latex shiner.
- Apply with a lint-free cloth or sponge.

## 3. Cleaning
- Wash your garment in lukewarm water with a mild, oil-free soap or specialized latex cleaner after every wear.
- Rinse thoroughly.
- **DO NOT** machine wash, tumble dry, or dry clean.

## 4. Drying
- Pat dry with a soft towel and hang to dry on a plastic or wooden hanger (avoid metal as it can rust and stain the latex).
- Ensure the garment is completely dry before storing.

## 5. Storage
- Store in a dark, cool place away from direct sunlight (UV rays cause "latex rot").
- Avoid hanging light-colored latex next to dark-colored garments to prevent color transfer.
- Use a garment bag or keep in a plastic bag to prevent sticking.

## 6. Safety Warning
- **Latex Allergy**: If you experience any skin irritation, discontinue use immediately. This product contains natural rubber latex.
