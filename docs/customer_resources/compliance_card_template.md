## SIDE B: Essential Care Instructions (The "Back")

**KEEP THIS CARD FOR FUTURE REFERENCE**

Latex is a unique material that requires specific care to maintain its shine and integrity.

### 1. Dressing
- Use **silicone-based lubricant** or **talcum powder**. 
- Never pull on the material with your fingernails.
- Keep away from oils, perfumes, and metal jewelry.

### 2. Shining
- Your garment arrives in a matte state. Use a specialized **silicone latex shiner** to achieve a high-gloss finish.

### 3. Cleaning & Storage
- **Hand wash only** in lukewarm water with mild soap.
- **Air dry completely** (avoid metal hangers).
- **Store in a dark, cool place** inside a plastic or garment bag. Keep light and dark colors separate.

---

**WARNING: LATEX ALLERGY**
This garment contains natural rubber latex which may cause allergic reactions. If irritation occurs, discontinue use immediately.

---

### Tips for a Professional Finish:
- **Paper Stock**: Use a heavy cardstock (14pt or 16pt) with a matte finish to contrast with the shiny latex.
- **Discreet Compliance**: By including your CA Number here, you meet the legal requirements of the *Textile Labelling Act* for garments where a permanent label is unsuitable.
- **QR Code**: Consider adding a QR Code on Side B that links directly to your digital care guide (`customer_resources/latex_care_guide.md`).
