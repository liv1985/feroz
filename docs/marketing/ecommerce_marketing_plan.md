# Feroz Fashion - Ecommerce & Marketing Strategy

## 1. Shopify Store Configuration (Canada-Specific)

### Shipping Profile
- **Local (Canada)**: Use **Canada Post** or **Chit Chats** integration.
- **US/International**: DHL Express for high-value latex items.
- **Rates**: Offer "Free Canadian Shipping" on orders over $200 to increase AOV.

### Tax Configuration
- **GST/HST**: Set up tax overrides for each province (e.g., 5% GST for AB, 13% HST for ON).
- **Duties**: Inform international customers that they are responsible for local duties.

### Product Page Essentials
- **Video Content**: Showing the "movement" and "shine" of the latex.
- **Size Guide**: Crucial for latex as it has limited stretch compared to spandex.
- **Care Link**: Link to the `latex_care_guide.md` on every product page.
